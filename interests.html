Coding

I only got into coding during June of last year. I was fascinated by the many things bots could do on Discord such as play music, minigames and even set reminders. I was bored one day and decided to watch a tutorial on how to make a Discord bot. I quickly learned the javascript coding language and started making the bot. After pulling an all-nighter, i finished a basic bot that me and my mates can use. Over time, I've added many more features to make our lives easier. My end goal is to make a bot that can do so many things that it replaces every other bot on Discord.
